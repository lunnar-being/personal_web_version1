Phase 1: Summer 2020 of the Industrial Energy Transformation Fund (IETF) opened in June 2020, and the competition ran from 20 July to 28 October 2020.

This page lists the first tranche of winners from this Phase and provides short summaries of each.

Subject to contracts being signed, these 13 projects will between them receive £16.4 million of IETF funding, which will go towards project costs totalling £48.6 million.

The winners include entries for both the Energy Efficiency Deployment competition and the Studies competition.

The second and final tranche of winners from the Phase 1 Summer 2020 competition will be announced later this year.

The Phase 1: Spring 2021 competition is open to applications until Wednesday 14 July 2021.
