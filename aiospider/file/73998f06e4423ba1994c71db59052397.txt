          Details


          

    
            
The CMA hosted a digital event on 3 March 2020 to explore how technology is changing the business models and investment strategies of firms in the digital economy and what this means for regulation.

The event gave viewers the opportunity to hear from a global roster of speakers including tech-centred business leaders, academics, investors, market analysts and heads of international competition agencies.

The event was live-streamed on YouTube from 11.00 am on Tuesday 3 March.
