    Domestic abuse: specialist sources of support
    
        HTML
    


  



  


        
          Details


          

    
            
If you want to access help specifically to cater to your background and needs or are experiencing a specific kind of abuse, the organisations in this guidance might be able to help.

It also includes information if:


  you need help for children and young people
  you need information about economic abuse, technological abuse, welfare benefits or housing advice
  you don’t have settled status in the UK
