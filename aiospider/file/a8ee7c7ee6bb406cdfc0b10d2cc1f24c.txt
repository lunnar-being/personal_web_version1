A specialist infrastructure engineer is a focused role that has specific and relevant capabilities to a particular area of infrastructure expertise.

This role is part of the Digital, Data and Technology Profession in the Civil Service.

Introduction to the role of specialist infrastructure engineer

The role fulfils a particular need where there is demand in an organisation. The individual job descriptions may be quite different depending on the demand in the organisation.

Specialist roles include but are not limited to:


  network engineer
  cloud service specialist
  data centre specialist
  security infrastructure
