This report argues that the success of the new UK National Cyber Force (NCF) will be determined by the quality of the leadership, strategy, structures and processes that shape its growth and operational use.The report places the creation of the NCF in the wider context in which cyber operations have become an increasingly prominent feature of competition between state and non-state actors.
