BEIS, NLF and EDF Energy (EDF) have agreed revised arrangements to deliver safe, low cost, more efficient and cost-effective decommissioning of EDF’s fleet of 7 Advanced Gas Cooled Reactor (AGR) stations.

See the factsheet for more detail.
