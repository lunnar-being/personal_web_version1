          Details


          

    
            
This guide will help you fill in the correct boxes on your tax return when you make a claim for capital allowances.

This helpsheet gives information about:


  capital allowances
  plant and machinery allowances
  plant and machinery used only partly for business purposes
  disposing of equipment
  cars
  accounting periods that are different from the tax year
  starting in business
  ceasing your business
  partnerships
  capital allowances you can claim if you’re an employee
  Business Premises Renovation Allowance
  Flat Conversion Allowance
