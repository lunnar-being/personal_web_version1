
      


  
    


  
  
    DRIVEN - ESF High Level Skills programme
  

  
  
  
    
  Grants and support to help SMEs across Derbyshire and Nottinghamshire to recruit and retain talented students and graduates. 

  



  
    
      
  
      From:
      
          Department for Business, Energy & Industrial Strategy

      
      Published
      16 April 2020
  

    
    
    
  






  
      
    
      Type of support: 
      
Finance, Grant, Expertise and advice

      Business stage: 
      
Start-ups (1-2 years trading), Established

      Industry: 
      
Agriculture and food, Business and finance, Construction, Education, Health, Hospitality and catering, IT, digital and creative, Life Sciences, Manufacturing, Mining, Real estate and property, Science and technology, Service industries, Transport and distribution, Travel and leisure, Utilities providers, Wholesale and retail

      Number of employees: 
      
0 to 9 employees, 10 to 249 employees

      Region: 
      East Midlands

          How much you can get

£1,000 funding per graduate recruited for 400 hours or more and / or circa £1,000 for fully funded 80 hour internship.  No limits on multiple awards.

What you can get

Grants and support with recruitment including:


  free advertising
  job advert creation
  job promotion
  sifting
  interview support


Organiser

University of Derby
