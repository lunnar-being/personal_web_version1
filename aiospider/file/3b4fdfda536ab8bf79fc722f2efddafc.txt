    Retained Block Exemptions
    
        HTML
    


  



  


        
          Details


          

    
            
Seven EU block exemptions were retained in UK law at the end of the transition period, along with their expiry dates.

The Competition and Markets Authority has announced it is reviewing the vertical agreements block exemption, which expires in May 2022. The other block exemptions will be reviewed in due course.
