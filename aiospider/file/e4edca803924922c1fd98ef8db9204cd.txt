The Science and Innovation Network Country Snapshot for Hungary provides information on the local Science and Innovation landscape, UK priorities and successes. It offers a snapshot of information under the headings: Science & Innovation Landscape; UK Science & Innovation, and Science and Innovation Network recent success stories and future activities. There is also a list of Science and Innovation Network contacts for further enquiries.
