Former Health, Science, and Technology Advisor at Department of State’s Crisis Management and Strategy within the Office of the Secretary.  Received Distinguished Honor Award for work on the COVID-19 pandemic.  The Senate unanimously passed bipartisan Resolution 567 commending the largest repatriation effort in U.S. history.  
Former Strategic Advisor to the Energy Sciences Area of Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory.
Former CEO of the biotech Bikanta, Board Member of the International Biomedical Research Alliance.
Post-doctoral fellowships at the National Cancer Institute and National Heart, Lung and Blood Institute.  Orloff Technical Advance Award.  
Research and 16 patents in nanomedicine and the early detection and treatment of disease.
Worked on various science outreach and national policy initiatives, including the National Nanotech Initiative, Nano Task Force, and Cancer Moonshot Initiative.
Marshall Scholar, National Institutes of Health-Oxford Scholar in Biomedical Sciences, American Association for the Advancement of Science (AAAS) Science & Technology Fellow, Helen Grenga Outstanding Woman Engineer Award, Georgia Tech Council of Outstanding Young Engineering Alumni Award.
