Award Number: DE-EE0009068CX(s) Applied: A9, B3.6Building Technologies OfficeLocation(s): COOffice(s): Golden Field OfficeThe U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) is proposing to provide funding to Simple Homes to develop a novel system for the design, off-site manufacture, and delivery of energy efficient single family homes. A central component of the project would be the development of an automated robotic production platform for use in the manufacturing of wall panels. A pilot version of the production platform would be developed, assembled, and tested.
