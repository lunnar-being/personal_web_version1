          Details


          

    
            
Local AAA screening providers should print out this card and use it at screening clinics when taking the verbal consent of the men being screened. It is available in English and 10 other languages.

An HTML version of the card is provided (in English) which can be printed out in a larger print size, or read using screen reader technology.


Contact the PHE Screening helpdesk with any queries about this publication, making sure you include its full title.
