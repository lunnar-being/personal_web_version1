          Details


          

    
            
Use this list if you’re a trader that’s not established in the EU, to see if the business that stores your goods in the UK is registered with the Fulfilment House Due Diligence Scheme (FHDDS).

Contact the Excise Helpdesk if you have any questions about the FHDDS.
