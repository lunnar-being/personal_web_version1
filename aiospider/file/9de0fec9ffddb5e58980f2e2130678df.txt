          Details


          

    
            
The Data Science Accelerator programme aims to help central and local government workers develop their data science skills. Participants work on their own project for one day a week over 3 months, supported by a mentor.

Email any questions to data.science.accelerator@ons.gov.uk.

You can read our privacy notice to find out about how we handle your personal data.
