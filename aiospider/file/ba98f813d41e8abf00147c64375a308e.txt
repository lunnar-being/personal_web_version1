    Japan-UK Joint Committee on Cooperation in Science and Technology: joint statement on meeting, 20 October 2021
    
        HTML
    


  



  


        
          Details


          

    
            
The UK and Japanese governments held the 11th Joint Committee Meeting on Science and Technology virtually on 20 October.

Discussions focused on:


  reviewing existing research and innovation cooperation, including in infectious diseases and regenerative medicine
  practical ways to deepen research cooperation through strategic cooperation and by promoting bottom-up research collaborations
  supporting business innovation partnerships
