          Details


          

    
            
The Emergency Services Network will be the new communication system used by the 3 emergency services and other public safety users in the UK.

In the above document you will find more information about ESN and the progress of the programme delivering it, including:


  the partners working on ESN

  how ESN is being piloted and tested
  
ESN’s mobile coverage
  devices that will be used to access the ESN
