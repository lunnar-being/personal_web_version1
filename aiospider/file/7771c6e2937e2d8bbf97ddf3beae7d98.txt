          Details


          

    
            
The publications provide information about the ‘strength in numbers’ framework.

This includes guidance on:


  the principles behind the Department for Transport’s (DfT’s) analytical assurance framework
  quality assurance processes for models
  registers of the DfT’s business-critical analytical models


This guidance is for DfT officials. We have published this to aid working with our partners and as part of our response to the Macpherson review.

The Strength in Numbers report, and the DfT register of business-critical models 2014 to 2017 were previously published in PDF format. These files are available on the  UK Government Web Archive.
