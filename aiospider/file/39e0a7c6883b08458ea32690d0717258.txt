          Details


          

    
            
To apply for Authorised Economic Operator status you will need to gather information about your business before you start your application.

This guidance will tell you the information you need to apply for:


  customs simplification status
  security and safety status
  both


Depending on the answers you give on your application form some of this information may not be relevant to you.
