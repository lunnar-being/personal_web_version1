This document explains the specific benefits for consumers and business of the UK-Australia Free Trade Agreement.

It contains an overview of the following sectors:


  agriculture, food and drink
  manufacturing
  automotive
  digital and data
  professional and business services
  financial services
  transport services
  telecommunications
  creative
  consumer goods and fashion
  small and medium sized enterprises (SMEs)
  life sciences
  chemicals
