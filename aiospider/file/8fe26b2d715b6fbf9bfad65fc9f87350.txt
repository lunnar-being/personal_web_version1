    Chief Scientific Advisers and their officials: an introduction
    
        PDF, 410KB, 31 pages
    


  



  


        
          Details


          

    
            
This document describes the roles and responsibilities of departmental CSAs and their supporting teams (CSA officials), and puts these in the context of the wider science advisory processes across government. It gives an introduction to existing guidance and networks for providing high level scientific and engineering advice to government.
