          Details


          

    
            
A collection of guidance showing how public sector organisations in the UK should use the cloud.

The collection includes guidance on how to:


  enable cross-functional collaboration throughout the cloud lifecycle
  realise best practice cloud service usage
  maximise commercial, technical, security, and people capabilities


The guidance also includes a number of case studies and blog posts as examples of how public sector organisations are currently using the cloud.
