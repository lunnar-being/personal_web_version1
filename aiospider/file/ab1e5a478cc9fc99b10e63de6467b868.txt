          Details


          

    
            
The guidance is for:


  local authorities
  school leaders
  school teachers
  governing bodies and their representatives


It relates to local authority-maintained schools and academies in England.

Methodology

This guidance will help local authorities and schools understand how the grant is calculated for 2022 to 2023.

Allocations

We have published indicative allocations of the schools supplementary grant at local authority level, presented along with final figures from the 2022 to 2023 dedicated schools grant (DSG) allocations that were published in December 2021. Finalised allocations of the schools supplementary grant will be published in spring 2022.

Conditions of grant

The conditions of grant will be published in spring 2022.

Further details

You can find out more about the dedicated schools grant (DSG) settlement for 2022 to 2023 following the autumn 2021 spending review.
