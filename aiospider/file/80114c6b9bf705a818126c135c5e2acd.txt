In Turkey, consumers’ income and household finances have been affected by COVID-19. A majority of Turkish consumers are concerned about personal health, the economy, and the duration of the crisis. Both the observed effects and the uncertainty around the situation are manifesting as decreased spending across most categories. A spike in online spending on household essentials and entertainment is expected to continue, at least in the short term.These exhibits are based on survey data collected in Turkey from April 7–9, 2020.
