The Zero Emission Vehicles Transition Council (ZEVTC) 2022 action plan sets out how the Council will work together on the following high priority areas throughout 2022:


  charging infrastructure
  
CO2 or fuel efficiency standards and regulations
  heavy duty vehicles
  supporting a global transition


About the Zero Emission Vehicles Transition Council

ZEVTC was formed in November 2020. Its objective is to accelerate the pace of the global transition to zero emission vehicles. The Council is made up of governments and representatives from some of the world’s largest and most progressive automotive markets. At their meeting on 10 November 2021, the Council welcomed the US becoming co-chair of the ZEVTC with the UK.
