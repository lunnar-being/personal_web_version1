Award Number: DE-EE0008517CX(s) Applied: A9, B3.6, B1.31Bioenergy Technologies OfficeLocation(s): AZOffice(s): Golden Field OfficeThe U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) is proposing to provide funding to Arizona State University (ASU) to design, develop, fabricate and test membrane carbonation delivery systems for photo-bioreactors and raceway pond algae cultivation systems. The membrane carbonation (MC) delivery systems would boost microalgae growth productivity. The project would be completed over three Budget Periods (BPs), with a Go/No-Go Decision Point in between each BP.
