          How much can you get

Vouchers for up to £1200 towards costs.

What you can get

Businesses and households can apply for a voucher of up to £1200 towards the cost of a choice of mobile signal booster options from a registered supplier. The value of the voucher will be dependent on the type of technology most suitable for the premises.

Suppliers will be able to advise on the most appropriate option.

Businesses and residents will be required to make a contribution to cover the cost of installation.

Organiser

Connecting Devon and Somerset (CDS), Somerset County Council.
