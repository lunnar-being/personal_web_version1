Given the choice of in-person, remote, and e-commerce channels, purchasers have shown they want them all.McKinsey’s research on B2B decision makers confirms that omnichannel is not simply a trend, nor a pandemic workaround—rather, it is a critically important fixture for B2B sales globally.
            
            
            
            
            
            
            
            
    Omnichannel isn’t going away. And that’s a good thing. B2B sales organizations that lean into this shift and enable integrated interactions across the buying journey could turn the learning curve of the last 12 months into a new trajectory for growth.
