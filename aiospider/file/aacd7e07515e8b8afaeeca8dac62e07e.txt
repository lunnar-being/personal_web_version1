In the US alone, an estimated 40 million people may face hunger in 2021; Black households experience food insecurity at about 2.5 times the rate of White households in this country, and Latino households at about 2 times the rate. On #WorldFoodDay—and every day—access to safe, nutritious, and affordable food is an imperative, particularly for vulnerable populations.
