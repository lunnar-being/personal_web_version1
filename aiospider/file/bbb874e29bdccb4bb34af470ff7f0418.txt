This report describes what the government has done since the Automated and Electric Vehicles Act in 2018.

This act gave the government power to expand and improve the national electric vehicle charging infrastructure. The aim is to have a charging network that is convenient, easy to access and ready to support the mass adoption of electric vehicles.
