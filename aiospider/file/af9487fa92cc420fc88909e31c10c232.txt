          Details


          

    
            
The UK has one of the most vibrant and productive life science sectors in the world. Its integrated life sciences and healthcare system places the patient its heart, and supports innovation at every stage. Partners work together to support a research and innovation culture that spans sectors and geographies.

A wide range of UK organisations can give support, advice or useful information for each development stage of your life sciences product or service.

This includes:


  research and development
  development and translation
  regulation and assessment
  delivery, supplying NHS and patients
  launching global access
