Award Number: DE-EE0008907CX(s) Applied: A9, B3.6Bioenergy Technologies OfficeLocation(s): MTOffice(s): Golden Field OfficeThe U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) is proposing to provide funding to Montana State University (MSU) to develop tools and techniques for the optimization of corn stover feedstock processing during preprocessing and conversion processes.
