          Details


          

    
            
Learn how eligible EU, EEA, Swiss citizens, and their family members can make a late application to the EU Settlement Scheme, now the deadline of 30 June 2021 has passed. These factsheets include information on reasonable grounds for late applications, the application process, and the support available.
