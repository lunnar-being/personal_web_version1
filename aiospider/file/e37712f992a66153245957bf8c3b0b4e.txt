          Details


          

    
            
Vehicle operators use IT systems and software to help manage their drivers and vehicle fleets.

To join the Driver and Vehicle Standards Agency (DVSA) earned recognition scheme for vehicle operators, you need IT systems and software that can report on some important performance measures.

The IT systems and software on this list work with the DVSA earned recognition scheme for vehicle operators.
