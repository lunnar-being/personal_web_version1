In Argentina, consumers’ prevailing sentiment is uncertainty. As Argentine consumers have mixed feelings about economic recovery, they are cutting back on spending and becoming more mindful how they spend their money. Consumers are shifting to online purchases within entertainment, food delivery and takeout, and consumer electronic categories.These exhibits are based on survey data collected in Argentina from September 1–11, 2020.
