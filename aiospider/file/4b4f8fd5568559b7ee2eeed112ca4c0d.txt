
      


  
    


  
  
    Lichfield District Start Up Grant Scheme
  

  
  
  
    
  A grant for people in Lichfield looking to start a business.

  



  
    
      
  
      From:
      
          Department for Business, Energy & Industrial Strategy

      
      Published
      18 June 2021
  

    
    
    
  






  
      
    
      Type of support: 
      Grant
      Business stage: 
      Not yet trading
      Industry: 
      
Agriculture and food, Business and finance, Construction, Education, Health, Hospitality and catering, IT, digital and creative, Life Sciences, Manufacturing, Mining, Real estate and property, Science and technology, Service industries, Transport and distribution, Travel and leisure, Utilities providers, Wholesale and retail

      Number of employees: 
      0 to 9 employees
      Region: 
      West Midlands
    
  


      
    


      
        

    
          Who it’s for

Lichfield residents who are redundant or currently economically inactive and starting a business in the current financial year.

How much you can get

A one-off grant of £3,000 for start-up costs such as:


  One item of capital equipment (excluding, laptops, mobile phones and tablets)
  Marketing
  Professional qualifications or training


Applicants must first go on the Enterprise for Success programme’s free two-day workshop.  Further details are available on the provider’s website.
