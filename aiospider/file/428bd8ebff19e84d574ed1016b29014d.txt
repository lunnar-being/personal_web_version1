          Details


          

    
            
HMRC publishes the Employer Bulletin 6 times a year, giving employers and agents the latest information on topics and issues that may affect them.

The bulletin is only available online. You can register for HMRC’s employer email alert service to receive emails from HMRC which tell you when the latest issue is available.

You can download and read the bulletin on screen or print it off, it’s compatible with most screen reading software packages.
