Award Number: DE-EE0009383CX(s) Applied: A9, A11, B3.2Solar Energy Technologies OfficeLocation(s): TXOffice(s): Golden Field OfficeThe U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) is proposing to provide funding to Tietronix Software, Inc. to develop the techniques, algorithms, and software to enable concentrated solar power (CSP) plant operators to efficiently improve solar field performance. This would be accomplished by using a non-intrusive optical measurement method to collect data that would be used for algorithm and software development.
