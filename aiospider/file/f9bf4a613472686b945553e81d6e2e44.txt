Award Number: DE-EE0009033CX(s) Applied: A9, B3.6Geothermal Technologies OfficeLocation(s): SDOffice(s): Golden Field OfficeThe U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) is proposing to provide federal funding to RESPEC Company LLC(RESPEC) for the design, development, fabrication, and laboratory testing of a directional cooling tool for measuring the in situ stress state in geothermal reservoirs. Project work would occur at RESPEC's office and laboratory (Rapid City, SD), University of Wisconsin-Madison (Madison, WI), and Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory (Berkeley, CA). Any work proposed to be conducted at a federal facility may be subject to additional NEPA review by the cognizant federal official and must meet the applicable health and safety requirements of the facility.
