          Who it’s for


  London based SMEs
  Start-up and established businesses


Maximum employees

249

What you can get

1 to 1 business support to discuss business challenges, including:


  mentoring
  coaching
  accountability


Workshops and events, including:


  forming a 90 day action plan
  sales and marketing
  how to access funding
  getting ‘fit to supply’ and ready to bid for and win contracts
  access to buyers and contract opportunities
