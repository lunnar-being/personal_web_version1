Award Number: DE-EE0009282CX(s) Applied: A9, B3.6Bioenergy Technologies OfficeLocation(s): OHOffice(s): Golden Field OfficeThe U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) is proposing to provide funding to Ntre Tech LLC to design, develop, fabricate, and test a low-emission, efficient residential wood-fired central heater.
