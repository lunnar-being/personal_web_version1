  This service is for software vendors who are providing digital technologies under the Help to Grow: Digital scheme.

Sign in to your Help to Grow: Digital account to:


  update and complete your product information
  add and manage users



This service is only for vendors who have been accepted onto the Help to Grow: Digital scheme.


 Start Now 

Before you start

You must activate your account before you can use this service. Use the link provided in the activation email sent to you on 25 October 2021.

To sign in you’ll need your:


  email address
  password


If you’ve forgotten your password, you can reset it using the email address you gave when you activated your account.

Help and guidance

If you have any questions, contact the Help to Grow: Digital team at vendors.helptogrow@beis.gov.uk.
