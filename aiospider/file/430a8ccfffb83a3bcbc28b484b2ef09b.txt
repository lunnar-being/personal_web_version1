          Details


          

    
            


The programme will support collaborative projects with our international partners which will enhance UK science, security and trade.

The funding call is structured into themes, with proposals needing to fall within one or more themes in order to be selected. Details of these themes and all other information you will need in order to be able to submit a proposal can be found in the call documents. The deadline for applications is 12 noon on 29 October 2020.
