Setting out the definition and scope of ‘technology’ for export licensing requirements.

This applies for military and dual-use technology falling within the scope of:


  the Export Control Order 2008

  
Retained Council Regulation (EC) No. 428/2009 (England, Wales, Scotland only)
  
Council Regulation (EC) No 428/2009 (Northern Ireland only)
