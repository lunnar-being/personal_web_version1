Award Number: DE-EE0009455CX(s) Applied: A9Building Technologies OfficeLocation(s): NYOffice(s): Golden Field OfficeThe U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) is proposing to provide federal funding to the Interstate Renewable Energy Council (IREC) to develop, launch, and promote educational solutions on solar, energy storage systems, electric/alternative vehicles, and grid-interactive efficient building technologies. These resources would be made available to safety officials and related audiences by online training through an educational clearinghouse website.
