This note provides:


  an explanation of the Specialist Employability Support programme and its aims, including detail on providers that deliver the programme nationally in England, Scotland and Wales
  a description of the process and support provided on the Specialist Employability Support programme
  the methodology used to compile the data used in the official statistics
