          Details


          

    
            
This privacy notice describes how the FCDO’s Protocol Directorate, in particular the Diplomatic Missions and International Organisations Unit (DMIOU) and Ceremonial Unit (CU) processes personal data. The DMIOU and CU processes data for diplomatic and consular staff in foreign diplomatic missions and consular posts in the UK, and officials posted to international organisations in the UK. The notice explains how we will use your personal data, and what your rights are in relation to our use of your personal data.


See also our Personal Information Charter.
