          Details


          

    
            
This privacy notice describes how the Forced Marriage Unit (FMU) processes personal data. The FMU is a joint Foreign, Commonwealth & Development Office and Home Office unit that processes data to provide support to victims and potential victims of forced marriage. The notice explains how we will use your personal data, and what your rights are in relation to our use of your personal data.


See also the FCDO’s Personal Information Charter and Home Office’s personal information charter.
