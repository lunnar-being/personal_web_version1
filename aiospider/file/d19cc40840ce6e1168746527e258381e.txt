          Who it’s for

You can get help from expert staff if you’re a:


  sole trader
  start-up
  microenterprise
  entrepreneur
  small and medium enterprises (SME)
  charity
  social enterprise
  person needing business information


What you can get

You can access a wide range of business information resources, including:


  general start-up advice
  information on market research
  national and international coverage for statistics
  company data
  business news
  business seminars and workshops on business topics, such as online marketing, pricing strategies, networking skills.


Organiser

Small Business Research + Enterprise Centre – City of London Corporation
