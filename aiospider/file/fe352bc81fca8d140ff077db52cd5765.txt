          Details


          

    
            
The purpose of this statutory guidance is to help trustees and scheme managers of defined contribution workplace pension schemes.

It affects pension schemes that fall within the scope of the Occupational and Personal Pension Schemes (Disclosure of Information) (Statements of Benefits: Money Purchase Benefits) (Amendment) Regulations 2021.

The guidance helps trustees and scheme managers to understand how to comply with those regulations, where they apply, when producing simpler annual pension benefit statements for their members.
