          Details


          

    
            
Following our consultation on e-scooter rental trials, this guidance document informs local areas and e-scooter operators about next steps in the design, implementation and management of e-scooter trials, covering:


  background
  timescales
  proposed regulatory changes to allow trials
  eligibility and trial requirements
  designing and agreeing proposals
  monitoring and evaluation, and DfT data requirements
  governance and reporting
