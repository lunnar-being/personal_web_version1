          Details


          

    
            
This guidance sets out the methodology Export Control Joint Unit (ECJU) uses for data collection and compilation of its strategic export controls statistics. These reports cover export licensing decisions made by Her Majesty’s Government (HMG).

Methodology and quality control procedures follow the UK Statistics Authority’s code of practice for official statistics.

The guidance covers:


  the data sources used
  collection methodology
  classification and definitions
  data quality standards
