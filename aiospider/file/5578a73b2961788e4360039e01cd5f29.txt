    UK Authorised Recognised Organisations (ROs)

The six classification societies authorised as UK Recognised Organisations are:


  
    American Bureau of Shipping (ABS)
  
  
    Bureau Veritas SA (BV)
  
  
    Nippon Kaiji Kyokai (ClassNK)
  
  
    DNV
  
  
    Lloyd’s Register (LR)
  
  
    RINA Services S.p.A (RINA)
  


These organisations are authorised in accordance with the:


  
    International Maritime Organization’s (IMO) Code for Recognized Organizations (“the RO Code”) (MSC.349(92) and MEPC.237(65)); and
  
  
    The UK Merchant Shipping (Recognised Organisations) (Amendment) (EU Exit) Regulations and MSN 1672 (Ship Inspection and Survey Organisations).
