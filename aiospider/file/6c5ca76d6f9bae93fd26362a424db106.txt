This guidance assists people in implementing and complying with the Syria (United Nations Sanctions) (Cultural Property) (EU Exit) Regulations 2020.  It covers the prohibitions and requirements imposed by the regulations.  It also provides guidance on best practice for:


  complying with the prohibitions and requirements
  enforcing them
  circumstances where they do not apply


This guidance should be read alongside more detailed sanctions guidance published by the Department for International Trade (DIT).
