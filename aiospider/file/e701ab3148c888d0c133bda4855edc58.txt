The following bereavement guide has been prepared for the convenience of British nationals who may require these services and assistance in Russia.

Includes information on:


  what to do when someone dies in Russia
  burial and cremation
  returning bodies to the UK (repatriation)


See also what to do when someone dies: death abroad.
