    Contain Outbreak Management Fund: 2020 to 2021
  

  
  

  
    
  The Contain Outbreak Management Fund (COMF) provides funding to local authorities in England to help reduce the spread of coronavirus and support local public health.

These documents set out guidance for English local authorities relating to the Contain Outbreak Management Fund (COMF) during financial year 2020 to 2021, with associated grant determination letters.

See also guidance for COMF for 2021 to 2022.
