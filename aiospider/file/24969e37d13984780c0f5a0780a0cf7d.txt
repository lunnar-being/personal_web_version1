This guidance is for decision makers working on energy and sustainability projects, broader estates management, and finance, in public sector organisations such as:


  local authorities
  schools
  NHS Trusts
  emergency services


It includes a selection of case studies to help public sector organisations better understand how they can benefit from smart meters.
