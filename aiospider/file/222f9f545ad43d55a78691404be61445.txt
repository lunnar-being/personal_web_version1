A technical specialist architect is a focused role that has specific and relevant capabilities relevant to a particular area of architectural expertise.

It fulfils a particular need where there is demand in an organisation. The individual job descriptions may be quite different depending on the demand in the organisation.

This role is part of the Digital, Data and Technology Profession in the Civil Service.
