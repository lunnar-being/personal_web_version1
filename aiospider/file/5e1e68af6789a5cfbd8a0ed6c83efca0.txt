  Support and grants for SMEs to help them to reduce carbon emissions.

  



  
    
      
  
      From:
      
          Department for Business, Energy & Industrial Strategy

      
      Published
      6 February 2020
  

    
    
    
  






  
      
    
      Type of support: 
      
Finance, Grant, Expertise and advice

      Business stage: 
      
Start-ups (1-2 years trading), Established

      Industry: 
      
Construction, Hospitality and catering, IT, digital and creative, Life Sciences, Manufacturing, Real estate and property, Science and technology, Service industries, Transport and distribution, Travel and leisure, Utilities providers, Wholesale and retail

      Number of employees: 
      
0 to 9 employees, 10 to 249 employees

      Region: 
      East Midlands

          How much you can get

Between £1,000 and £20,000; 40% of project cost.

Who it’s for

Some sectors are excluded such as social welfare, education, banking and insurance; check with the team for further details.

What you can get

Advice and grant support for reducing business carbon emissions, including:


  more efficient lighting
  insulation
  heating/cooling systems
  equipment
  renewable energy
  resource efficiency
  waste reduction
  low carbon transport


Organiser

Derby City Council, Derbyshire County Council, and the University of Derby.
