    ENCA: Featured tools for assessing natural capital and environmental valuation

Featured tools are selected based on the following criteria:


  cross-cutting in ecosystem services and habitats
  developed or funded by government
  helpful in valuing or assessing natural capital


The following tools are featured:


  Tool Assessor
  Defra Biodiversity Metric
  Environmental Benefits for Nature Tool
  Natural Capital Atlases
  Natural Capital Register and Account Tool
  Managing for Ecosystem Services Evidence Review Toolkit
  Local Environment and Economic Development (LEED) Toolkit
  Environmental Valuation Reference Inventory (EVRI)
  Natural Environment Valuation Online (NEVO)
  Outdoor Recreation Valuation Tool (ORVal)
  Woodland Valuation Tool


Disclaimer

This is not an exhaustive list of tools. Tools referenced in other parts of ENCA but not included in this list may also be appropriate for the user’s needs.

Defra does not endorse how you use these tools, nor does it provide a comparative assessment. It is your responsibility to assess the relevance and robustness of any particular tool you may wish to use. The commentaries provided aim to support this judgement.
