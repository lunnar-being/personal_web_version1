          Details


          

    
            
These privacy notices explain how MCA collects, uses and stores your personal information during certain activities, or when you use its services.

The MCA personal information charter explains more about your rights over your personal data.

Prior to 5 December 2021, the content of these notices was included within the MCA personal information charter where they are now listed instead.

              7 December 2021
              Edited Emergency response notice - In certain rescue circumstances it may be necessary for us to share your data with global search and rescue authorities... and any other declared rescue facility.

If you contact us directly to report an incident, we will ask you for your contact details which may be shared with our coastguard rescue officers/volunteers... this may include relevant medical details at the time of the incident which will ensure appropriate rescue.
