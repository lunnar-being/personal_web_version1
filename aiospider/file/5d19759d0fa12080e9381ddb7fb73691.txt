The TSST Programme has now closed.

From April 2021, training for non-specialist teachers who currently teach maths or physics will be delivered by the maths hubs and the Stimulating Physics Network (SPN).

Support for non-specialist maths, physics and languages teachers has been aligned with existing curriculum programmes and their delivery partners. Contact them directly to access support.


Support for non-specialist maths teachers

From April 2021, the National Centre of Excellence in the Teaching of Mathematics (NCETM) will work with maths hubs to deliver a programme aimed at non-maths teachers currently teaching maths: specialist knowledge for teaching mathematics - secondary.

For more information see the National Centre of Excellence in the Teaching of Mathematics (NCETM) website.

Support for non-specialist physics teachers

From April 2021, the Institute of Physics (IOP) will work with the Stimulating Physics Network (SPN) to deliver a programme aimed at non-physics teachers currently teaching physics: specialist knowledge for teaching physics programme.

For more information contact the Stimulating Physics Network.

Support for non-specialist languages teachers

From December 2021, the National Centre for Excellence for Language Pedagogy (NCELP) will provide online continuing professional development on modern foreign languages pedagogy and is available to all specialist and non-specialist teachers. Extensive resources (including screencasts) are available now on their website.

For more information contact the National Centre for Excellence for Language Pedagogy.

Other opportunities
You can:


  contact the Association for Language Learning which supports and represents language teachers from all sectors
  read A guide to STEM continuing professional development (CPD) opportunities for teachers  (PDF, 274KB, 15 pages) for opportunities in science, technology, engineering and mathematics teaching
  search for other subject association and professional organisations who may offer continuing professional development (CPD) opportunities for teachers
  contact your local higher education institutes (HEI) for subject-specific professional development for teachers
