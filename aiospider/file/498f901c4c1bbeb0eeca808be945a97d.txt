The Government Veterinary Services (GVS) profession includes a wide variety of roles and specialisms and is spread across geographically diverse UK locations. It is made up of government vets with a background and formal
veterinary education, who work in a range of specialist, deep specialist, policy, analytical and operational roles.

The GVS profession workforce strategy outlines 3 key priorities for 2019 to 2022:


  attract and retain talented individuals to the GVS profession
  improve the skills and capability of GVS professionals across government to enable progression to Senior Civil Service level
  improve learning and development opportunities for GVS professionals across government
