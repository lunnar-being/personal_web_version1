
      


  
    


  
  
    Low Carbon Dorset
  

  
  
  
    
  Free support to help businesses in Dorset reduce their carbon emissions, improve energy efficiency and aid the development of new low carbon products

  



  
    
      
  
      From:
      
          Department for Business, Energy & Industrial Strategy

      
      Published
      14 May 2021
  

    
    
    
  






  
      
    
      Type of support: 
      
Grant, Expertise and advice

      Business stage: 
      
Start-ups (1-2 years trading), Established

      Industry: 
      
Business and finance, Construction, Education, Health, Hospitality and catering, IT, digital and creative, Life Sciences, Manufacturing, Real estate and property, Science and technology, Service industries, Transport and distribution, Travel and leisure, Utilities providers, Wholesale and retail

      Number of employees: 
      
0 to 9 employees, 10 to 249 employees

      Region: 
      South West
    
  


      
    


      
        

    
          Who it’s for


  SMEs operating within Dorset
  Public-sector organisations located in Dorset
  Legally constituted voluntary or community organisations located within Dorset
  Registered charities located within Dorset


The scheme cannot support agriculture and activities that are directly related to primary processing (for example, purchasing equipment to fell and process timber into woodchip).

What you can get

A range of free services including -


  
    Technical advice to explore your low carbon project ideas
  
  
    Grants for up to 40% of project costs, between £5,000 and £250,000
  
  
    Low carbon workshops and events
  
  
    A detailed Energy Survey of your business
  
  
    Networking opportunities
  


The programme is funded by the England European Regional Development Fund.
