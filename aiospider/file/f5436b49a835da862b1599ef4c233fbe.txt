Prototype Coastal Acoustic Buoy for Offshore Wind unit ready to be deployed for field testing. On the left is the surface buoy with a data cable that relays information from the lander (on the right), which features three underwater microphones, battery storage, and onboard signal and data processers. SMRU Consulting staff prepare the CABOW unit for deployment. This real-time acoustic monitoring platform can be used by offshore wind energy developers to minimize construction impacts on North Atlantic right whales.
