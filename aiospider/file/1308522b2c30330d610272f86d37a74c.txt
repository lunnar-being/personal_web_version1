
      


  
    


  
  
    The Business Hothouse
  

  
  
  
    
  Free workshops, business mentoring, networking and grant funding for people starting or growing a business in the Coast to Capital LEP area.

  



  
    
      
  
      From:
      
          Department for Business, Energy & Industrial Strategy

      
      Published
      11 May 2021
  

    
    
    
  






  
      
    
      Type of support: 
      
Finance, Grant, Expertise and advice

      Business stage: 
      
Not yet trading, Start-ups (1-2 years trading), Established

      Industry: 
      
Business and finance, Construction, Education, Health, Hospitality and catering, IT, digital and creative, Life Sciences, Manufacturing, Mining, Real estate and property, Science and technology, Service industries, Transport and distribution, Travel and leisure, Utilities providers

      Number of employees: 
      10 to 249 employees
      Region: 
      South East
    
  


      
    


      
        

    
          
How much you can get


  Up to £5,000 grant for eligible start-up businesses
  Up to £170,000 for those established and growing


What you can get


  a range of workshops and bootcamps
  ongoing 1-2-1 business mentoring sessions
  online resources to help you start or grow your business
  grant funding from the Invest4 Grant Fund


Support is delivered by a range of partners and funded through the European Regional Development Fund (ERDF) and Local Authorities in the area.

For further details on eligibility criteria and to express interest in accessing the programme, please contact the team.
