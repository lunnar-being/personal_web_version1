The Disability Unit is seeking 5 senior business people to become Disability and Access Ambassadors. They will be champions for disability and access inclusion in their sector for 1 to 3 years.

Read the overview and candidate information pack to find out more.

If you would like to apply, submit the expression of interest form by 11:59pm on Sunday 3 October 2021. You can download the form as a Word document or OpenDocument.
