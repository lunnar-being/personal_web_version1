Award Number: DE-EE0008382CX(s) Applied: A9, B3.6Solar Energy Technologies OfficeLocation(s): NHOffice(s): Golden Field OfficeThe U.S. Department of Energy is proposing to provide federal funding to Brayton Energy to design a full-scale integrated thermal energy storage heat exchanger and related test articles. During the project Brayton Energy (Brayton) would demonstrate a heat exchanger technology that can be integrated with fixed-position thermal energy storage (TES) media.
