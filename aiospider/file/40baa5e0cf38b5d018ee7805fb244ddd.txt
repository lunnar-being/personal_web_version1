          Who it’s for

The programme provides residents of Bedford Borough Council, Central Bedfordshire, Milton Keynes and Northamptonshire with access to free business start-up support.

Support can include:


  1-to-1 business advice
  webinars and workshops
  small business start-up grants
  business incubation workspace
  specialist social enterprise support


Organiser

Wenta in partnership with The University of Bedfordshire and The University of Northampton.

The programme is part-funded by the European Regional Development Fund.
