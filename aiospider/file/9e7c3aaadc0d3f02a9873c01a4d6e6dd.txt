          Details


          

    
            
If you are working on a research grant or award from a funder approved by UK Research and Innovation (UKRI) you may be eligible for Global Talent.

Use this list to find out which endorsed funders are eligible under the route.

You must also be working at an approved institution in the UK.

Read more about Global Talent UKRI endorsement
