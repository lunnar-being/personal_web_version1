          Details


          

    
            
Defra lists recognised EU breed societies for bovine, ovine, porcine and equine and caprine species.

Zootechnical rules and standards support trade in:


  purebred breeding cattle
  sheep and goats
  pigs and hybrid pigs
  equines
  genetic material of all these animals


Read about the work of the Farm Animal Genetic Resources Committee.

Contact: fangr@defra.gov.uk
