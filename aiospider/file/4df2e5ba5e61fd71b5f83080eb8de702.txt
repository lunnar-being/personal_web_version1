Who it’s for

Start-up; established SMEs and freelancers based in England working on projects, products or services that have a direct benefit to the Southeast LEP area (Kent, Essex, East Sussex).

What you can get


  Free access to high-tech creative facilities, studios and working spaces in Canterbury, Folkestone and Maidstone
  Product development expertise from researchers and industry specialists
  Innovation voucher competitions offering longer-term access to facilities


The scheme is supported by the European Regional Development Fund and delivery partners.

Organiser

London South Bank University in partnership with Canterbury Christ Church University, Creative Folkestone, Maidstone Studios and Screen South.
