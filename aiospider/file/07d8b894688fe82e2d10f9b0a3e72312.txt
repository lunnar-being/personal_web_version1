On December 18, 2020, the U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) Office of Enterprise Assessments’ Office of Enforcement notified Honeywell Federal Manufacturing & Technologies (FM&T), LLC, the managing and operating contractor for the Kansas City National Security Complex, of its intent to investigate potential noncompliances with Honeywell FM&T’s implementation of DOE’s 10 C.F.R. Part 851, Worker Safety and Health Program at the Kansas City National Security Complex.
