
      


  
    


  
  
    E-business Programme - Enfield, Waltham Forest and Redbridge
  

  
  
  
    
  Advice and training to help businesses improve their use of digital tools and technology to increase their sales and grow their business.

  



  
    
      
  
      From:
      
          Department for Business, Energy & Industrial Strategy

      
      Published
      16 December 2021
  

    
    
    
  






  
      
    
      Type of support: 
      Expertise and advice
      Business stage: 
      
Start-ups (1-2 years trading), Established

      Industry: 
      
Agriculture and food, Business and finance, Construction, Education, Health, Hospitality and catering, IT, digital and creative, Life Sciences, Manufacturing, Real estate and property, Science and technology, Service industries, Transport and distribution, Travel and leisure, Wholesale and retail

      Number of employees: 
      
0 to 9 employees, 10 to 249 employees

      Region: 
      London
    
  


      
    


      
        

    
          Who it’s for

Eligible applicants must be running businesses:


  trading for over 6 months
  which can produce the latest management accounts, bank statements etc.
  located in one of the following London boroughs: Enfield, Waltham Forest or Redbridge
  with fewer than 251 employees


What you can get

Business Advisers and Digital experts, together with workshops and webinars, will help you understand how to incorporate digital tools and technology in your business to:


  Increase sales and attract new customers
  Enhance online presence
  Maximise your website’s search engine ranking (SEO)
  Harness the power of email marketing and social media
  Use online sales platforms
  Tap into new sales with ecommerce / online payments
  Improve your customer service and Customer Relationship Management (CRM) systems
  Maximise your business efficiency, save time and money
  Find safer security and back up systems


Support is driven by the needs of the business.
