For nearly  10 years, McKinsey has studied the relationship between diversity and performance. Time and time again, the findings have been consistent: inclusion matters. Companies with  diverse executive teams show greater likelihoods for achieving financial outperformance. And to get to such progress, leaders will need both ambition and resilience.In this second episode of our Women in STEM series, we talked with , co-founder of Entrepreneur First; , VP of Digital for Commercial at Moderna; , chair of the board of the NYU Tandon School of Engineering; , CEO of Digital14; and , CIO of Liberty Mutual on building diverse teams—why it matters and how they do it.
