          How much you can get

A £2,500 innovation voucher to access expertise in research institutes (50% of project costs)

Who it’s for

Established SMEs based in:


  Birmingham
  Solihull
  Redditch
  Bromsgrove
  Wyre Forest (Kidderminster)


Priority sectors


  advanced manufacturing (including automotive and aerospace)
  low carbon
  ICT
  digital & creative
  life science


Organiser

Aston University, in partnership with Birmingham City University.
