          How much you can get

Up to £2,000

Who it’s for

Businesses in the Elmbridge area, including:


  Claygate
  Cobham
  Molesey
  Oxshott
  Stoke D’Abernon
  Long Ditton
  Thames Ditton
  Hinchley Wood
  Walton on Thames
  Weybridge


What you can get

Elmbridge-based businesses can apply for a one-off grant of up to 50% of the cost of business projects that:


  benefit the local economy
  attract the public to local services and amenities


The type of projects the scheme supports include:


  street furniture and street scene improvements
  bringing a shop that has been empty for at least three months back into commercial use
  improving shop fronts
  learning and skills or training initiatives
  initiatives to promote community improvement projects


Start-ups can get up to £1,000. High street retail businesses upgrading to an e-commerce product sales website can get up to £1,500.

Organiser

Elmbridge Borough Council
