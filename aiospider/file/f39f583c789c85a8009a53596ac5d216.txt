          Details


          

    
            
All UK Government departments are formally required to converge on this Global Design for HR. The design may equally be used across the wider public sector.

The Global HR Reporting Standards are currently being updated, so have been removed from this site temporarily, however if you wish to access them to progress your current ERP implementation please login to OneGovCloud. A newly updated version will be published here on gov.uk in the near future.

The Global HR Design core documents:

  Overview, Global Design Principles and HR Process Taxonomy - introduction, principles and scope for the Global HR Design
  User Journeys Suite - persona-based journey maps setting out the vision for high quality HR services and business outcomes
  Level 3 Process Splits (RACI) - indicative flow of process and roles across the taxonomy
  HR Service and Technology Standards - quality, process-aligned KPIs, data fields and self service capabilities
  Leading Practice Features - the measures, enablers and principles needed for quality HR service delivery, categorised into bronze, silver and gold features
  Appendix - Glossary


The Global HR Design Library additional guides:

  Guide: The Impact on the HR Operating Model
  Guide: The Service Delivery Model
  Guide: The Role of the Line Manager
  Guide: An Introduction to HR Enabling Technologies
  Guide: Organisation and Position Management

The following materials have been developed to assist  all public sector HR teams with cloud programmes - large or small:

  Journey to the Cloud - A Guidebook: Tools Appendix
