          How much you can get

From £1,000 to £15,000, 30% intervention rate.

Who it’s for

SMEs looking to collaborate on research and innovation projects. Businesses must also be based along the Oxford to Cambridge Arc area (formerly the Cambridge – Milton Keynes – Oxford corridor).

What you can get


  Fully funded expert consultancy to help you develop your innovation plans
  Grant opportunities between £1,000 & £15,000 to implement your idea
  50% funding up to £10,000 toward a 1 year Masters by Research programme to incorporate your innovation
  Co-funding of up to £10,000 for a full 3-year Ph.D. to undertake a distinct research project and implement ideas


Organiser

University of Bedfordshire
