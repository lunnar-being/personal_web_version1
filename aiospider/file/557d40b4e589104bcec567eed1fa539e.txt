          Details


          

    
            
The Windrush Day Grant Scheme provides funding to communities looking to celebrate, commemorate and educate about the Windrush Generation and their contribution. Applications for funding will remain open until 10 March 2021.

Online bidder workshops will be taking place on 2 February and 11 February 2021 to provide information to organisations looking to apply to the scheme. Details on the workshops and how to register can be found on the registration page.
