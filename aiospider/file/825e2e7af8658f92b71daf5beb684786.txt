          Details


          

    
            
If you visit our diplomatic missions and residences, the FCDO processes your personal data for security reasons.

During the coronavirus pandemic, host governments may ask our embassies, high commissions, consulates and international missions to share visitor lists with them for public health management reasons. This privacy notice explains how we manage your data.
