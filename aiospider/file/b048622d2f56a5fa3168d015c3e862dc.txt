          Details


          

    
            
IETF Phase 1: Spring 2021 provided up to £40 million of grant funding for energy efficiency projects and studies. Innovate UK and BEIS jointly delivered this phase of the competition for England, Wales and Northern Ireland.

Phase 1: Spring 2021 project types

Phase 1: Spring 2021 provided funding for:


  projects deploying technologies that improve the energy efficiency of industrial processes
  feasibility and engineering studies into energy efficiency and deep decarbonisation measures for industrial processes


Competition dates

The Phase 1: Spring 2021 competition ran from 8 March until 14 July 2021. Applications are currently being assessed.

Eligibility

The competition was open to businesses of any size registered in England, Wales or Northern Ireland within the following categories:


  those within the manufacturing SIC codes 10-33
  data centres


Funding

The funding was allocated as grants within these thresholds:


  
    
      Funding applied for
      Minimum threshold per application
      Maximum threshold per project
    
  
  
    
      Energy efficiency deployment projects
      £100,000
      £14 million
    
    
      Engineering studies
      £50,000
      £14 million
    
    
      Feasibility studies
      £30,000
      £7 million
    
  


The minimum thresholds were lowered in response to feedback received during Phase 1: Summer 2020.

Get in touch

If you have questions about the IETF or would like to discuss your application, please contact ietf@beis.gov.uk.

If your site is based in Scotland, you can apply for the Scottish Industrial Energy Transformation Fund (SIETF): contact IETF@gov.scot for more information.
