Sarah Domnitz, PhD

													

									

			

		

	



				








2017-2019 AAAS Science & Technology Policy Fellow at the Department of Defense.
Embassy Science Fellow at the U.S. Embassy in Paris, France in 2019.
Former co-director of consensus studies on adult hearing loss and organ transplantation at the National Academies of Sciences, Engineering, and Medicine (NASEM).
Recommendations from the NASEM consensus study on hearing loss (along with recommendations from a report by PCAST) informed the drafting of the Over-the-Counter Hearing Aid Act of 2017, which became law as part of the FDA Reauthorization Act of 2017.
Former director of the NASEM Forum on Aging, Disability, and Independence.
Graduate research focused on proteins that facilitate chromosome movement during cell division.
