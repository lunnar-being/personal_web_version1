          Details


          

    
            
This privacy Notices describes how the FCDO’s Academic Technology Approval Scheme Unit processes personal data. It does this in order to consider applications for an ATAS certificate for study in the UK, for counter proliferation purposes.


See also our Personal Information Charter.
