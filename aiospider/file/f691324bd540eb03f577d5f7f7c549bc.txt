    FSR-C-116 Sexual assault examination: requirements for the assessment, collection and recording of forensic science related evidence
    
        PDF, 699KB, 50 pages
    


  



  


        
          Details


          

    
            
This standard sets out the accreditation and requirements for the forensic medical examination carried out routinely at designated sexual assault examination facilities. It includes the timescale for implementation of these requirements and an associated self-assessment check list to support organisations to assess their readiness state and identify their preparation gaps.
