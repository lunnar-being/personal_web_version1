          Details


          

    
            
This guidance from the Forensic Science Regulator provides details of the obligations placed on expert witnesses in the criminal justice system in England and Wales.

It provides information on the requirements imposed by:


  the statute
  case law
  criminal procedure rules
  criminal practice directions
