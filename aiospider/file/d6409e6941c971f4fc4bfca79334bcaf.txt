Award Number: DE-EE0009372CX(s) Applied: A9Solar Energy Technologies OfficeLocation(s): MTOffice(s): Golden Field OfficeThe U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) is proposing to provide federal funding to the National Center for Appropriate Technology to develop a web-based clearinghouse of agrivoltaic information, termed the Agri-Solar Clearinghouse. This clearinghouse would serve as an information-sharing, relationship-building, public communications hub, and professional network for the practice of co-locating agriculture and solar development.
