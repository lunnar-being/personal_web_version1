OIRA Reports in .XML Format





The information displayed elsewhere on Information and Regulatory Policy webpages is not presented in a format that allows download and analysis by members of the public. In keeping with OIRA’s policy of transparency and accessibility, the following reports are available in a machine-readable format.





These files are in XML (Extensible Markup Language), a language to describe structured data. OIRA has chosen the XML standard to ensure open access to this information. For more information on the XML standard, please see the
