Terms of reference for the independent union connectivity review into how connectivity across the UK can support economic growth and quality of life post-coronavirus (COVID-19).

The terms of reference include information on:


  context
  scope
  timings


The review will be chaired by Sir Peter Hendy CBE and was announced on 30 June 2020 by the Prime Minister.
