          Details


          

    
            
The Digital Imaging and Multimedia Procedure is a guide for those practitioners within the police and criminal justice system who are involved with the capture, retrieval, storage or use of evidential digital images, and associated audio and metadata, either generated by the police themselves or recovered from witnesses under the CPIA (Criminal Procedure and Investigations Act 1996).

You should read the main document in conjunction with the flowchart.
