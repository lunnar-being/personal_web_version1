In Spain, consumer optimism about the economy has doubled since February 2021, reaching 33 percent. Consumers indicated greater intent to spend, and almost half intended to splurge this year. At equal rates of 60 percent, Spanish consumers said they are omnichannel shoppers and have returned to participating in out-of-home activities (way up from 20 percent in February 2021). More than four in ten reported changing brands, mainly for price. Over one-third said more of their holiday shopping would be online in 2021 than in 2020, and about 40 percent said they would start shopping earlier.These exhibits are based on survey data collected in Spain from October 15 to 22, 2021.
