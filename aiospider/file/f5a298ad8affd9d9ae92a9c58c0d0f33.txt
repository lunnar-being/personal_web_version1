The Rt Hon Kwasi Kwarteng MP and H.E. Dr Majid Al Qasabi held the second meeting of the Economic and Social Pillar under the Strategic Partnership Council on 14 July 2021.

The joint statement outlines how the partnership will drive forward Economic and Social Pillar collaboration, which is split into 4 clusters:


  trade, investment and financial services
  education and healthcare
  energy, industry and environment
  culture, sport and tourism
