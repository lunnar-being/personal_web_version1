          Details


          

    
            
Joint Service Publication 539 is the policy for the prevention of climatic injuries (heat illness and cold injury) in the armed forces for activities including training, exercises and operations.

It also contains guidance on the initial medical management and treatment of climatic casualties.


JSP 539 only contains Treatment policy. JSP 375 Chapter 41 and Chapter 42 covers guidance on heat illness and cold injury prevention.
