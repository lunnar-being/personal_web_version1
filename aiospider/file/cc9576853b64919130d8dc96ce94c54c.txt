    Competition guidance for EUREKA collaborative R&D: advanced materials, March 2020
  

  
  

  
    
  UK registered businesses can apply for a share of up to £1 million for advanced materials R&D projects partnering with EUREKA country organisations, including South Korea.

          Details


          

    
            
To be eligible for funding from Innovate UK, your collaboration:


  must include at least one UK registered business of any size
  must include at least one eligible partner from one of the participating regions of the EUREKA call for proposals for joint R&D projects on advanced materials
  can include UK registered research organisations
  must follow the full process correctly


We strongly welcome partnerships with businesses from South Korea.

Please read the full competition scope before you apply.
