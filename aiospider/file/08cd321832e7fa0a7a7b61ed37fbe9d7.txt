  Part of the Midlands Engine Investment Fund, flexible loans to SMEs looking to grow and help drive their expansion plans across the Midlands Engine area.

  



  
    
      
  
      From:
      
          Department for Business, Energy & Industrial Strategy

      
      Published

      Type of support: 
      
Finance, Loan

      Business stage: 
      Established
      Industry: 
      
Business and finance, Education, Health, IT, digital and creative, Life Sciences, Manufacturing, Science and technology, Service industries, Transport and distribution, Wholesale and retail

      Number of employees: 
      10 to 249 employees
      Region: 
      
East Midlands, West Midlands

    
  


      
    


      
        

    
          Who it’s for

SMEs trading business to business across the Midlands Engine area, who are looking to grow but unable to raise all the finance required.

Most sectors are eligible with a few exceptions, further details are available from the provider.

What you can get

Flexible loans of £100,000 up to £1,500,000.

Finance can be provided to support growth plans and include working capital, asset purchase, product development, taking on new staff and marketing initiatives.

The lender is not able to re-finance existing debt.
