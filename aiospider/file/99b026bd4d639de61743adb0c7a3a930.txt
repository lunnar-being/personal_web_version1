Award Number: DE-EE0009271CX(s) Applied: A9, B3.6Bioenergy Technologies OfficeLocation(s): UTOffice(s): Golden Field OfficeThe U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) is proposing to provide funding to Utah State University (USU) to increase the economic feasibility and commercialization of algal energy and bioproducts technology. Specifically USU would improve the feasibility and readiness of the Rotating Algae Biofilm Reactor (RABR) and biofilm microalgae.
