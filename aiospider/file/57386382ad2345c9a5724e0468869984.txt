Award Number: DE-EE0009330CX(s) Applied: A9, B3.6Solar Energy Technologies OfficeLocation(s): OHOffice(s): Golden Field OfficeThe U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) is proposing to provide funding to Toledo Solar Inc. to design, develop, and fabricate semi-transparent photovoltaic (PV) window modules using a laser-ablation patterning process. Improvements would be made to the ablation process and to the time needed to complete production of highly efficient PV panels with desirable transparency.
