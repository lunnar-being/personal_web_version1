The Civil Service Leadership Academy offers a three part series of virtual workshops focused on leading in complexity.

This three part Leading in complexity series aims to support senior Civil Service leaders to the Cynefin Model as a framework to help us to understand how we can best respond in our environments.

Visit the CSLA programmes and events calendar for the full list of upcoming series dates
