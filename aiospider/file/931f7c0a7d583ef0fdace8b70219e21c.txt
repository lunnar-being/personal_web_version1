          Details


          

    
            
The Kickstart Scheme terms and conditions on this page are for employers and Kickstart gateways receiving the Kickstart Scheme grant from the Department for Work and Pensions (DWP).

Kickstart Scheme Grant Funding Agreement: Terms and Conditions for Employers

This sets out the terms and conditions between DWP and an employer who has applied directly to the Kickstart Scheme.

Kickstart Scheme Grant Funding Agreement: Terms and Conditions for Gateways

This sets out the terms and conditions between DWP and a Kickstart gateway taking part in the Kickstart Scheme.

Kickstart Scheme Terms and Conditions for Gateways and Employers

This sets out the terms and conditions which DWP requires to be in place between a Kickstart gateway and their employers.
