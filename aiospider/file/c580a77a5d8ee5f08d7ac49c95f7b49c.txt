              10 June 2021
              Published a revised 'background information document' and 'methodology and quality statement' (both now dated June 2021). The background information document has revised information about COVID-19 operational changes, and the methodology and quality statement has revised information about appeals data from the HM Courts & Tribunals Service (HMCTS). These have been revised to support the latest release of outcomes of Work Capability Assessments statistics (also published today, 10 June 2021).
            
            
              11 March 2021
              Published a revised background information document and methodology and quality statement (both now dated March 2021). These have been revised to support the latest release of outcomes of Work Capability Assessments statistics (also published today, 11 March 2021).
            
            
              14 January 2021
              Published a revised methodology and quality statement (now dated January 2021). Revised to support the latest release of outcomes of Work Capability Assessments statistics (also published today, 14 January 2020).
            
            
              12 March 2020
              Published a revised methodology and quality statement (now dated March 2020) in both HTML and PDF formats. Revised to support the latest release of outcomes of Work Capability Assessments statistics (also published today, 12 March 2020).
            
            
              13 December 2019
              Published a revised methodology and quality statement, now dated December 2019. Changes to document include an intention to review and revise the methodology for matching ESA-WCA appeals to ESA-WCA data. This includes information about the quality of the current matching process.
            
            
              5 August 2019
              Published a revised methodology and quality statement, now dated August 2019. This includes revised information about clearance times.
            
            
              13 September 2018
              Revised methodology and quality statement - dated September 2018. This includes new information on medical condition and International Classification of Diseases (ICD) codes.
            
            
              13 June 2018
              Revised methodology and quality statement - now dated June 2018. This includes information on new initial clearance times statistics and more geographical breakdowns covering Work Capability Assessment outcomes.
            
            
              14 September 2017
              Revised methodology and quality statement - dated September 2017. This includes information on new clearance times statistics, which were published for the first time on 14 September 2017.
            
            
              29 March 2017
              Revised version of Methodology and quality statement: ESA outcomes of Work Capability Assessment - the new version is dated March 2017.
            
            
              8 December 2016
              Published a 'Methodology and Quality Statement' (dated 2016). This single publication replaces the 2 documents 'Methodology statement: ESA outcomes of Work Capability Assessment' and 'Quality statement: ESA outcomes of Work Capability Assessment'.
            
            
              12 June 2014
              Published background information document.
            
            
              25 March 2014
              First published.
