          Who its for?

You must be a resident of Warwickshire, and have a business idea (or ideas).

What you can get

Free, local and specialist business support offering a range of workshops from industry experts to explore self-employment and being a business owner. Also offers access to a peer support network and start up finance.

Organiser

Coventry & Warwickshire Reinvestment Trust
