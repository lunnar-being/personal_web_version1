William Press, PhD, is a computer scientist, computational biologist, and astrophysicist who co-discovered the mathematical model for predicting the distribution of masses of galaxies throughout the universe. 





Researcher and manager across a broad range of physical and life sciences in universities and national laboratories, with experience in national security issues.
Vice-Chair of PCAST during the Obama-Biden administration.
Former Deputy Director for Science and Technology at Los Alamos National Laboratory (1998-2004).
Joint appointment as Professor of astronomy and physics at Harvard University (1976-1998).
Member of U.S. National Academy of Sciences and the current elected Treasurer.
Member of Governing Board of the U.S. National Research Council.
Past President of the American Association for the Advancement of Science.
