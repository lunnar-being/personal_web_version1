    Joint statement of the United Kingdom of Great Britain and Northern Ireland and the United States of America on cooperation in quantum information sciences and technologies
    
        HTML
    


  



  


        
          Details


          

    
            
This joint statement sets out a shared vision for continued cooperation on quantum information science and technologies between the UK and the US, including:


  promoting joint research and development efforts between national quantum programmes
  growing the global market
  providing training opportunities for scientists and engineers
