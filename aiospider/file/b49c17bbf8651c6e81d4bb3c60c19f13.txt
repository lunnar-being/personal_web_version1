          Details


          

    
            
This notice replaces the February 2018 edition of Notice 455a: Remote Gaming Duty.

It has been revised to give information about changes to RGD rates that came into effect on 1 April 2019, together with the changes to the treatment of RGD on freeplays which came into effect for all accounting periods beginning on or after 1 August 2017.

It includes guidance on UK persons, registration, keeping records, accounting periods, submitting returns and making duty payments to HMRC.
