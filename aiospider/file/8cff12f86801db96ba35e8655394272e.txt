The Department for Business, Energy and Industrial Strategy (BEIS) has provided £79,815 of funding for a gap analysis of health and safety standards for electricity storage technologies that are at or near deployment.


This competition is now closed. Frazer-Nash Consultancy have been awarded £79,815 to undertake the project.
