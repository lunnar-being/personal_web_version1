Award Number: DE-EE0007823CX(s) Applied: A9, B3.16Water Power Technologies OfficeLocation(s): WAOffice(s): Golden Field OfficeThe U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) is proposing to provide funding to the University of Washington (UW) to improve the quality of acoustic information collected from drifting hydrophones and acoustic instruments, known as the Drifting Acoustic Instrumentation SYstem (DAISY) while reducing the costs of deployment and analysis. DAISY was previously known as Acoustic Surface Wave Instrumentation Float with Tracking (A-SWIFT).
