          Who it’s for

All businesses in Essex at any stage, of any size and in any sector.

What you can get

Fully-funded one-to-one advice from a team of professional business advisers about a range of issues, including:


  marketing
  finance
  cashflow
  distribution
  sales
  premises
  employing people
  product development
  business planning
  business workshops
  innovation
  serviced offices
  virtual tenancy
  business incubation
  signposting to other resources and organisations


Organiser

Colchester Business Enterprise Agency (COLBEA)
