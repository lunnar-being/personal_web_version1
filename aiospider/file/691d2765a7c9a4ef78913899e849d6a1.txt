
      


  
    


  
  
    Small Growth Grants for Dorset
  

  
  
  
    
  Small grants towards projects over £4,000 for Dorset businesses with ambitions to grow. 

  



  
    
      
  
      From:
      
          Department for Business, Energy & Industrial Strategy

      
      Published
      17 August 2020
  

    
    
    
  






  
      
    
      Type of support: 
      Grant
      Business stage: 
      
Start-ups (1-2 years trading), Established

      Industry: 
      
Business and finance, Health, IT, digital and creative, Life Sciences, Manufacturing, Science and technology, Service industries, Transport and distribution, Travel and leisure, Utilities providers, Wholesale and retail

      Number of employees: 
      
0 to 9 employees, 10 to 249 employees

      Region: 
      South West
    
  


      
    


      
        

    
          Who it’s for

Dorset businesses trading more than 12 months, with ambitions to grow.  ERDF exclusions apply, please read the full website guidance before applying.

What you can get

A grant of between £1,000 - £5,000 for projects with a value of £4,000 or above.

Successful applicants are provided 25% of eligible project costs.

Examples of typical project costs include:


  Acquisition of plant, machinery, tooling
  Property improvements
  Outsourced specialist consultancy
  IT or digital infrastructure costs such as cloud-based technology
  Digital marketing and online content production
