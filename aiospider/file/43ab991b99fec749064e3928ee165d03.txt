Acquisition Letters (ALs) are issued under the authorities of the Senior Procurement Executives of DOE and NNSA. ALs are intended for use by procurement professionals of DOE and NNSA, primarily Contracting Officers, and other officials involved in the acquisition process. Only DOE and NNSA Contracting Officers make definitive interpretations of how ALs affect DOE and NNSA contracts and related procedures. ALs are effective upon issuance and until rescinded.The purpose of this Acquisition Letter is to provide a determination and findings authorizing a waiver to the usual requirement to synopsize in the Commerce Business Daily proposed contract options expected to be awarded to the National Academy of Sciences.
