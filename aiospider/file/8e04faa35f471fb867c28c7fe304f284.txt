The Regulators’ Pioneer Fund sponsors projects led by regulators or local authorities which aim to help create a UK regulatory environment that encourages business innovation and investment.

The competition for the new round of funding launched on 20 May 2021 and closed on 15 July 2021.

The Fund will invest £3.7 million in 21 selected projects. This documents provides further details about the selected projects.

If you require further information, please contact: regulators.pioneerfund@beis.gov.uk.
