          How much you can get

Debt Funds £50,000 - £300,000

Equity Funds £100,000 - £300,000

Who it’s for

Your company must base some its operations in Surrey or Hampshire parts of the Enterprise M3 area.

You can’t use this fund for


  refinancing existing shareholders
  ‘turnaround’ strategies, that is taking a poorly performing or failing business and improving it


Organiser

The FSE group
