          Details


          

    
            
NHS population screening: identifying and reducing inequalities has generic guidance and resources on tackling inequalities in all national NHS screening programmes.

The guidance in this publication is specifically to support providers, commissioners and other partners involved in the delivery of the 6 NHS antenatal and newborn (ANNB) screening programmes:


  fetal anomaly screening
  infectious diseases in pregnancy screening
  sickle cell and thalassaemia screening
  newborn and infant physical examination
  newborn blood spot screening
  newborn hearing screening


It includes information and resources on how to:


  identify inequalities
  reduce inequalities


It also includes links to other resources.


Contact the PHE Screening helpdesk with any queries about this publication, making sure you include its full title.
