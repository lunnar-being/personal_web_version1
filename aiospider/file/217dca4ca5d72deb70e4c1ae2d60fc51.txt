    G7 Digital and Technology - Ministerial Declaration
  

  
  

  
    
  The UK, Canada, France, Germany, Italy, Japan, the US and European Union have agreed to the G7 Digital and Technology Ministerial Declaration.

          Details


          

    
            
This G7 Ministerial Declaration and Annexes set out the G7’s commitment to working together across vital areas: internet safety; data free flow with trust; electronic transferable records; digital technical standards; digital competition and critical digital, telecoms, and ICT supply chains.
