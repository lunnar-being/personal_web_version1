The Electric Power Research Institute (EPRI), in collaboration with BWXT Nuclear, PTR-Precision Technologies, Bridger Welding Engineering, Rusach International, and Advanced Manufacturing Research Center, proposes to manufacture and assemble several critical sections of a 2/3-scale reactor pressure vessel (RPV) using various advanced manufacturing and fabrication technologies, including electron beam welding (EBW), diode laser cladding, powder metallurgy-hot isostatic pressing, and advanced machining.
