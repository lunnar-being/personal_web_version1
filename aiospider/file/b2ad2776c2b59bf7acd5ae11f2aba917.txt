          Details


          

    
            
These documents present the national screening standards for the NHS fetal anomaly screening programme (FASP).

The most recent standards apply to data collected from 1 April 2021. They replace previous versions.

See Population screening: our approach to screening standards for the consistent approach we take to screening standards across all 11 NHS screening programmes.
