          Details


          

    
            
This open general export licence (OGEL) allows, subject to certain conditions, the export or transfer of goods, software or technology from the UK to any of the countries listed in the licence.

This version of the licence is available for registration.

You will need to download the military goods, software and technology undertaking template to use this OGEL.

If you do not meet the conditions of this licence, you may be able to consider using the government or NATO end-use OGEL. This allows military goods to be exported to a government or a NATO organisation in any of the destinations or countries listed in the licence.

Classified goods

If the goods are classified, you are likely to require Form 680 clearance if you are planning to use this OGEL.

Reporting requirements
This OGEL is subject to reporting requirements under the open licence returns transparency initiative.

Revoked licences

The most recent revoked licence is available in the documents section.

You can find older revoked versions of the OGEL (military goods, software and technology) on the National Archives website.
