          Details


          

    
            
These privacy information notices have been produced by the Drugs and Firearms Licensing and Unit (DFLU) to assist licence/authority/registration holders and applicants to understand how and why DFLU collects/handles their personal data.

The stakeholder list gives a detailed breakdown of what information might be passed to in line with the privacy information notices.
